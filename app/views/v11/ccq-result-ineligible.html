How we calculated your client’s financial eligibility

This calculation was made using the rules for controlled work (opens in new tab) and the amendments to these rules (opens in new tab).

Guidance on determining financial eligibility for controlled work and family mediation can be found in the guide to determining controlled work (PDF, 599KB).

We’ve used the information you provided to compare your client’s income and outgoings. This allows us to calculate their monthly disposable income.
